Welcome!

The Department of Mathematics at Kyungpook National University (KNU) is committed to advancing fundamental and applied mathematical sciences. Our department actively conducts research in diverse areas such as algebra, geometry, analysis, and applied mathematics. With a vibrant academic atmosphere, we aim to cultivate creative problem-solvers who can contribute to both theoretical and interdisciplinary fields.

We encourage collaboration and innovation, fostering the next generation of mathematicians through rigorous education and research opportunities. Learn more about our research, people, and ongoing academic activities through this site.
