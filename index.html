Welcome!

I am currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Kyungpook National University

I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Wyoming in 2015, and my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Mathematics from Kyungpook National University, Daegu, South Korea, in 2006 and 2012, respectively.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Information Science and Technology, ShanghaiTech University, China. My research interests include matrix analysis, numerical linear algebra, large-scale data analysis, and their applications to machine learning, data mining, and communication.
